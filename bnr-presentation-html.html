03 — The Innovation

The AI Quote Tool:
friction removed, pipeline built.

The power washing industry runs on callbacks and estimates that take 24–72 hours. The AI quote tool inverts this entirely — a homeowner lands on the site, answers 4 questions about their property, and gets an accurate estimate in under 60 seconds. No phone tag. No friction. The prospect is qualified and primed to book before a competitor even calls back.

05 — The Opportunity

SEO score: 32/100.
Ceiling: unlimited.

The baseline audit revealed significant structural gaps — missing schema, thin location pages, zero backlink profile, and no AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) for AI search tools like ChatGPT and Perplexity. These aren't weaknesses. They're the roadmap. Every gap is a lever that, pulled, directly increases qualified inbound traffic.

Gap 01
Local Schema
Missing structured data for service area, business type, and reviews — invisible to Google's local pack.
Gap 02
Location Pages
No dedicated pages for The Woodlands, Spring, Willis — 3 high-intent markets within service range.
Gap 03
AEO Content
Not indexed by AI search engines. Zero presence when prospects ask ChatGPT for recommendations.
Gap 04
Backlink Authority
No local citations, directory listings, or partnership links to signal trust to Google.
